Our Story

Aromas From Heaven is a family-owned and operated candle factory based in Indianapolis, Indiana near the famous Indianapolis Speedway. We take pride in manufacturing a superior premium candle that provides a continuous fragrance. Each candle is custom blended, colored, and hand poured with the highest quality products on the market. Our candles burn cleanly and provide a wonderful fragrance throughout the entire life of the candle, right down to the bottom of the container. Our manufacturing process is designed so that the fragrance is just as strong towards the end of the candle as it is in the beginning. We use nothing but the best quality raw materials to make the highest quality candle that can be made. Each candle is hand poured and hand inspected. No automated machines are used. We are continually testing our products to ensure we maintain the highest quality product.

Aromas From Heaven also hand-crafts wax melts using a process that holds a significant amount of oil while maintaining a hardened exterior, making the fragrance last longer than other melts. Fragrance oils are also available. Time and time again, our customers have preferred our premium candles and melts over our competitors. With over 400 fragrances to choose from, our aromas are heaven sent!
